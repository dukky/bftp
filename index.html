bftp

Transpiler from brainfuck to c.

Build

gcc -O3 -std=c99 -o bftp bftp.c

Usage

./compile.sh hellobrainfuck.bf out

or

./bftp hellobrainfuck.bf | gcc -O3 -o out -xc -

./out : Hello World!
